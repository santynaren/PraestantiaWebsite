Highlights

ALSIM Operator

Orient Flight School is the first privileged operator of ALSIM simulator in India. ALSIM is a France based Flight Simulator manufacturing company. The Directorate General of Civil Aviation in India approves the Simulator for imparting basic and advanced training on instrument approach procedures, simulated emergency procedures etc.

Largest & Versatile Fleet

Orient Flight School has a fleet of twelve aircraft, four Cessna 152s, five Cessna 172s, a Piper Seneca III, a Beech craft King Air C90A and a Beechjet 400. Cessna 152 – Basic two seater VFR/IFR equipped trainer aircraft

Placements

Orient Flight School provides placement assistance for meritorious students including individual counselling. Students of OFS work in close association with their instructors towards the progress in their careers and also through the Career Advisory Service.

Courses Offered

Commercial Pilot Licence:(CPL)

DGCA approved CPL (IR) integrated course offering, 200 hours of flying , 350 hours of ground training supported by 20 hours on the simulator

Private Pilot Licence:(PPL)

This is the first step to a professional pilot licence, which entitles you to fly an aircraft on your own. PPL is ideally suited for aviation enthusiasts and hobby pilots. This course consists of 50 hours of flying on single engine aircraft and 250 hours of ground training.

Student Pilot Licence:(SPL)

Students will be offered training in basic flying subjects such as, Theory of Flight, Air Regulations, Aviation Weather, Air Navigation, Aero Engines, Air Frames and Avionics. In addition, they will get 5 hours of flying, on the flight Simulator.
